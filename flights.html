Resouces for Flight Booking Options

Following are the list of flight booking options and websites that are best suited for availing cheap flights that are not listed on google search and some airlines offer student discounts.

Information

Flight booking is one of the trickest task while travelling to any country as you not only require a good flight but also at affordable prices. And for that timing should be optimum.

Websites

Following are list of websites which one should check while booking their flights.

  • kiwi.com
  • Emirates {offers student discount}
  • SkyScanner.com
  • kayak.com

Tricks

In order to avoid random price change, use VPN and incognito mode while visiting these sites to book flight.

Preferably book flights that have thier departure on thurdays and tuesday, with booking day as Saturday.
